About TALENTWOOD

Powered by Saregama's 130+ years of music industry expertise and AI technology

TALENTWOOD is Saregama's revolutionary platform that democratizes music talent discovery in India. By combining crowdsourced competitions with AI-powered evaluation and industry expertise, we're creating the next generation of Indian music superstars.

Our Mission

To discover and develop India's first digitally-born global music superstar while providing equal opportunities to musicians across the country.

Our Vision

To become India's leading platform for music talent discovery, creating authentic voices that represent India's diversity on the global stage.
